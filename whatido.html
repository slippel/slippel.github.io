The number of visits will depend on your goals.

In a single session, we can discuss your current diet, your goals and questions, and one or two specific recommendations.

I also offer pantry sweeps, as well as guided grocery shopping trips to help you identify what are the best-quality products for the price. I like to keep your budget in mind, and make eating healthy affordable and attainable!
