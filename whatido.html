The number of visits will depend on your goals. In order for you to experience lasting change, it is highly recommended that we visit once per week for at least 5 sessions, and then every 2 weeks beyond.
In a single session, we can discuss your current diet, your goals and questions, and one or two specific recommendations.
In multiple sessions, I become your accountability partner. Your goals will be monitored, and recommendations adjusted as needed.

I also offer pantry sweeps, as well as guided grocery shopping trips to help you identify what are the best-quality products for the price. I like to keep your budget in mind, and make eating healthy affordable and attainable!

Cooking Instruction

I teach beginners and those who are already fairly comforatable in the kitchen how to prepare delicious meals that support your overall well-being.
I love to address specific health concerns and dietary needs and preferences, including therapeutic diets!
Cooking classes are also great for partners, families, and friends to get together and have a good time.

Prices may vary based on your preferences and other factors, such as subject matter and setting.
If you are interested in a series of cooking classes, or learning in conjunction with nutrition counseling and/or personal chef service, prices will be discounted.

Cooking classes take place at Nourish Northwest, and last about 2 hours.
